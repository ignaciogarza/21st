The future is now

Accelerated changes in technology, migration, demographics, climate and pandemic such as COVID-19 are reshaping the social, economic and political order. This new configuration brings with it new demands on the construction of individuals. How they are formed in this dynamic environment will determine whether these changes are transformed into opportunities that can be effectively capitalized on to positively transform countries and the region as a whole.
In response to these new challenges, the IDB launched the initiative The Future is Now: Transversal Skills in Latin America and the Caribbean in the 21st Century with leading international experts in October 2019 in Panama
LEARN MORE

Watch the event here

The event was attended by leading international experts and organized in collaboration with Virtual Educa Parlamento Latinoamericano. The Coalition was also launched at this meeting, which promotes the development and strengthening of #skills21 that seeks to support the implementation of a new generation of education and training policies in Latin America and the Caribbean.
